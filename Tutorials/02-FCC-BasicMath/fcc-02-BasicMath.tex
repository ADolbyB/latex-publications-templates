\section{Percents / Per Milles / Per Myriads (Basis Points):}

$$ 35\text{\textpertenthousand} = 3.5\text{\textperthousand} = 0.35\% $$

$$ 3.5\text{ mills} = \frac{3.5}{1000} = 0.0035 = \frac{35}{10000} = 3.5 \times 10^{-3}  = 0.35\% $$

$$ 3.5\text{ mills} = \frac{\$3.50}{\$1{,}000} = \frac{7}{2000} = \frac{35}{10{,}000} = 3.5 = 10^{-3} = \left(\frac{7}{20}\right)\% = 3.5\text{\textperthousand} $$

$$ 3.5\text{ mills} = \frac{3.5}{10^3} = \frac{7}{2 \cdot 10^3} = \frac{35}{10^4} = 3.5 \times 10^{-3} = \frac{7}{20}\times 10^{-2} = 35\text{\textpertenthousand} $$

$$ .35\% \equiv 3.5\text{ mills} \equiv 35\text{ basis points}$$
